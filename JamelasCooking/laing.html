Laing

Laing originated from the Philippines' Bicol region, the southernmost part of Luzon. Because of its great taste and budget-friendly cost, this dish became well-loved. Laing or Natong is a spicy dish that has coconut milk based with dried taro leaves.

Video Guide

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. dried taro leaves
  • 10 pieces shrimp
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons shrimp paste
  • 1 piece onion minced
  • 5 cloves garlic crushed
  • 3 thumbs ginger Julienne
  • 6 pieces Thai chili pepper
  • 3 ounces pork fat
  • Ground white pepper to taste
