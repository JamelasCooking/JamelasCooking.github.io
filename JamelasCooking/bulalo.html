Bulalo

Bulalo originates from the Southern Luzon region of the Philippines. Many theories have evolved regarding its origin. One theory says that this type of dish originated in Batangas where you find many variations of Bulalo. Another theory says that it came from Tagaytay which has a large population of cows

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shank
  • 1/2 cabbage whole leaf individually detached
  • 1 bundle bok choy (petchay)
  • 2 cobs corn each cut into 3 parts
  • 2 Tablespoons whole peppercorn
  • 1/2 cup green onions cut unto 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 onion
  • 34 ounces water
  • fish sauce to taste optional
