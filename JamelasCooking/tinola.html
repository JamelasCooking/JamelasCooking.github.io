Tinola

Tinola is an authentic Filipino dish that was invented around the 1800s and is referenced in Jose Rizal's novel Noli Me Tangere where Capitan Tiago ordered the said dish after having not eaten it for a long time because of his extended stay in Europe.

Video Guide

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 36 ounces Tubig bigas
  • 1/2 piece green papaya cut into wedges
  • 1 tablespoon garlic minced
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • 1 thumb ginger cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 cup Hot pepper leaves
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
